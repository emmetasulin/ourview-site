FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need to be tech-savvy to use ourview?
Not at all. We handle the entire setup for you. All you need to do is share some basic info about your business — hours, services, FAQs — and we take it from there. If you can read an email, you can use ourview.
Will customers know they're talking to AI?
The AI responds naturally and professionally. Most customers won't notice a difference. You can customize the tone and style to match your brand, and the AI is trained specifically on your business's information.
What happens to my existing phone number?
You keep your number. We either set up call forwarding to our system, or give you a tracking number that routes calls the same way. Either way, your number doesn't change for your customers.
What platforms does the DM automation support?
Growth and Pro plans include Instagram and TikTok. Both require a business/creator account on each platform. Setup takes about 10 minutes once you're onboarded.
Can I cancel anytime?

What does early access mean?
